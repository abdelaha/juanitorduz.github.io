About

I am a math PhD student at Humboldt Universität zu Berlin and member of the Berlin Mathematical School. In this same university I obtained a master degree (Mathematics) in 2014 . Here I share my experience. Before comming to Berlin I did two bachelor degrees: Mathematics and Physics at Universidad de los Andes.

My research interests are differential geometry, topology and geometric analysis, in particular variants of the Atiyah-Singer Index Theorem for singular spaces. Currently I'm more interested around topics in data analysis, statistics and machine learning.
